## What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a conversational AI model developed by OpenAI that can understand and generate human-like text. Built on the GPT architecture, it can help with writing, coding, research, brainstorming, and much more.

## Creating Your Account

1. Visit [chat.openai.com](https://chat.openai.com)
2. Sign up with your email or Google/Microsoft account
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile setup

ChatGPT offers a free tier that's powerful enough for most use cases. The Plus subscription ($20/month) gives you access to GPT-4, faster responses, and priority access during peak times.

## Basic Prompting Techniques

The quality of ChatGPT's output depends heavily on how you phrase your prompts. Here are some key principles:

### Be Specific

Instead of "Tell me about marketing", try "Give me a 5-point social media marketing strategy for a small SaaS startup targeting B2B customers."

### Provide Context

Tell ChatGPT who you are and what you need: "I'm a freelance graphic designer. Write a professional email template I can use to follow up with potential clients after our first meeting."

### Use Role-Playing

"You are an experienced career coach. Review my resume below and suggest 5 specific improvements..."

## Advanced Features

- **Code Interpreter**: Upload files and let ChatGPT analyze data, create charts, and more
- **Custom Instructions**: Set persistent preferences for how ChatGPT responds
- **Voice Conversations**: Available on the mobile app for hands-free interaction

## Best Practices

1. Verify important information independently
2. Use clear, structured prompts
3. Iterate on responses — ask follow-up questions
4. Export and save important conversations
5. Respect ethical boundaries and privacy
