# The Site Is Overseas-Facing — English Only

## Background

When writing article LET-47, I created both a Chinese and an English version. The reviewer pointed out that `ai-tools-101.com` is currently an overseas-facing site and does not support Chinese content.

## Content

- **Current site language**: English only.
- **Target audience**: Global / English-speaking.
- **All new articles must be written in English.** Do not create Chinese, Japanese, or other non-English content unless explicitly requested.
- If the source material (e.g., a viral post from Chinese social media) is in Chinese, translate it to English in the article — do not publish the original Chinese version.

## Conclusion

Always publish content in English on this site. If bilingual content is needed in the future, it should be explicitly requested by the project owner.
